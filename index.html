Family US Roadtrip 2025

Who's Going

  • You – Parent
  • Partner – Parent
  • Child 1 – Age 9
  • Child 2 – Age 13
  • Sister-in-law – Adult
  • Nephew – Age 16

Itinerary

  • July 29 (Tuesday)
    Fly to Las Vegas – Arrive from Amsterdam.
  • July 30 (Wednesday)
    Zion National Park – Drive from Las Vegas. Stay at Red Cliff Lodge.
  • July 31 - Aug 1 (Thu - Fri)
    Zion National Park – Explore hikes and Virgin River.
  • Aug 2 (Saturday)
    Page, AZ – Antelope Canyon, Horseshoe Bend.
  • Aug 3 (Sunday)
    Grand Canyon / Williams – Visit Grand Canyon South Rim.
  • Aug 4 (Monday)
    Las Vegas – Meet sister-in-law and nephew. 3-night stay.
  • Aug 5 - 6 (Tue - Wed)
    Las Vegas – Explore city, shows, pool, family attractions.
  • Aug 7 (Thursday)
    Joshua Tree – Drive from Las Vegas. Stargazing & desert exploring.
  • Aug 9 (Saturday)
    Marina del Rey – Stay at Jamaica Bay Inn. Meeting until Aug 15.
  • Aug 15 (Friday)
    Santa Barbara / Ventura – Beach town overnight stay.
  • Aug 16 (Saturday)
    Springville (Sequoia NP) – Airbnb stay, see giant sequoias.
  • Aug 19 (Tuesday)
    Beverly Hills – Final days in LA.
  • Aug 21 (Thursday)
    Fly Home – Flight back to Amsterdam from LAX.
